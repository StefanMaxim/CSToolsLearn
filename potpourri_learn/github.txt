Github Webpages:

first, you need an index.md in your main branch accessible on the top layer.
From there, go to settings, Pages, and choose your publishing source, make it your main branch.
then it will publish the website

Whenever you recommit, Jekyll will rebuild your website from the markdown files in your directory.
